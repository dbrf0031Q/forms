How to buy a car

Search:

In Ukraine, the process of purchasing a car begins with thorough searching and selection. It is essential to determine your needs and budget; the chosen car should meet all the requirements. Utilize internet platforms, car dealerships, and recommendations from friends to gather maximum information about models and prices.

Legal Aspects:

After finding the desired car, pay attention to the legal aspects. Verify the presence of all necessary documents with the seller, including the technical passport and documents confirming ownership rights. Conclude the agreement, ensuring that you are familiar with all the conditions before signing the purchase and sale contract.

Registration and Inspection:

The final steps involve registering the car and undergoing a technical inspection. Contact the local State Auto Inspection office to process documents in your name as the owner. Then, proceed with the technical inspection to ensure your car is in proper condition. Once these stages are completed, you can enjoy your own vehicle on the roads of Ukraine.
